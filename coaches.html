Chris Russell
Chris has been playing chess competitively since he was 8 and was the British Under 16 champion in 2005.
He moved back to London in July 2017, having spent the previous five years as a secondary school maths teacher in Bristol.
Whilst at university Chris organised chess clubs in local primary schools through the Chess Rocks and Warwickshire Children's University schemes.
Qualifications
  1. 2017 ECF Accredited Coach
  2. 2016 Certified by Bristol Safeguarding Children Board on completion of Safeguarding Awareness Training
  3. 2014 Qualified Teacher Status
  4. 2013 PGCE in Secondary Mathematics from University of Bristol
Chess Achievements
  1. 2016 British Universities Chess Association Team Championships co-commentator with Phil
  2. 2014 Downend and Fishponds Club Champion
  3. 2009 World Circular Chess Champion
  4. 2008-10 University of Warwick Chess Club Executive Member
Chris has held a peak ECF grade of 209 and live FIDE rating of 2150
Phil Makepeace
Phil was England Under 18 Captain in 2007 having played in his first tournament at age 6
He started teaching chess full-time in 2013 and is also a cricket statistician in the summer.
Since moving to London in 2009, Phil has taight chess in nearly twenty primary and secondary schools and has been a resident coach in the Junior 4NCL since 2014.
Qualifications
  1. 2016 Certified by Camden Borough Council on completion of Safegurading Awareness Training
  2. 2014 ECF Accredited Coach
Chess Achievements
  1. 2017 Media and publicity manager at the British Chess Championships
  2. 2016 British Universities Chess Association Team Championships co-commentator with Chris
  3. 2013 Beat a grandmaster in a slowplay game for the first time
  4. 2010 Runner-up in British Under 21 Championship
  5. 2007 England Under 18 Captain
  6. 2006 & 2007 Southern Counties Junior Champion
Phil has held a peak ECF grade of 201 and live FIDE rating of 2116
